🍪 About Cookiebox!

🍪 What is Cookiebox?

Cookiebox is your trusted companion for discovering the finest cookies around! We taste, test, and review cookies from various stores, rating them especially on their milk-dipping quality. Whether you love a crispy dunk or a chewy bite, Cookiebox helps you find your next favorite.

🍪 Our Mission

Our goal is to make your cookie shopping easier, tastier, and a little bit more fun. We believe no cookie should be judged without a proper milk test — that's why every cookie we showcase comes with a detailed Dip Quality Rating. Cookies are meant to be enjoyed — and we’re here to guide you to the best!

🍪 How We Rate Cookies

Every cookie is judged on:

  • Flavor — How delicious it is at first bite.
  • Texture — Crispy, chewy, crumbly... the perfect mouthfeel.
  • Dunk Performance — Does it survive the milk dunk like a champion?
  • Overall Satisfaction — How happy it makes you!

Our unique Dip Quality Rating (0.5–5 scale) focuses on how well a cookie handles a good dunk in cold milk — because dunking should make a cookie even better, not ruin it!

🍪 Where to Buy

We’re not a store ourselves — we highlight where you can find these amazing cookies. You’ll find the shop name listed with each cookie. Support your local stores and discover hidden gems!

🍪 Who We Are

Cookie lovers. Milk dunkers. Crumb catchers. 🍪🥛
Cookiebox was started by a small team passionate about finding the best cookies for everyday moments, family gatherings, and cozy nights.
